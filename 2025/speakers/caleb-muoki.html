Caleb Muoki

Senior Platform Engineer

Muoki Caleb is a Senior Platform Engineer with over six years of experience in the tech industry. A technologist at heart, Muoki thrives at the intersection of infrastructure, developer tooling, and automation—building resilient systems that empower teams and scale with confidence. His professional journey spans cloud computing, CI/CD, cybersecurity, and infrastructure as code, reflecting a passion for engineering excellence across disciplines. Outside the 9-to-5, Muoki is just as hands-on—whether it’s trying to defy physics in 20-year-old project cars or cultivating mindfulness through Vipassana meditation. Always learning, always building, Muoki brings curiosity and intentionality to every facet of his life.

Talks by Caleb Muoki

Day 2
11:25 AM - 12:00 PM

DevOps is Dead, Long Live Platform Engineering

DevOps began with a promise—faster delivery, better collaboration, empowered teams. But over time, it became a catch-all job title weighed down by toil. This talk explores the rise of Platform Engineering as a true evolution: applying product thinking to infrastructure and internal tooling. We’ll explore how platform engineering enables scale, removes friction, and brings developer experience (DX) to the forefront—aligning engineering efforts with business outcomes.
