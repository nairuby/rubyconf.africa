THE ORGANIZER - AFRICA RUBY COMMUNITY
The African Ruby Community (ARC) is a community of 5000+ Ruby Developers developers (Engineers), entrepreneurs, designers and freelancers with chapters in Nairobi- Kenya, Kampala - Uganda, Kigali-Rwanda, and Dar es Salaam- Tanzania.
ABOUT THE CONFERENCE - RUBY CONF AFRICA 2018
On the 28th to 30th June 2018, leading Ruby developers from around the world will come together to share, inspire and learn. RubyConf Kenya is an inclusive conference and as such strives to be welcoming and accessible to as many people as possible.
