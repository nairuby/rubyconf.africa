ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to Ruby Mini Conf Africa 2021

This Mini-conference theme, “Innovate. Commercialize. Transform with Ruby,” emphasizes our dedication to promoting an innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem in Kenya and beyond. The summit aims to unite stakeholders—students, startups, and established companies—to showcase innovative ideas and entrepreneurial skills that tackle local, national, and global challenges.
  • Showcase and advocate for Open Source technologies as catalysts for advancement across Industry, Entrepreneurship, and Academia.
  • Foster discussions and explore how Agile methodologies can be adapted and optimized for sustainable development practices within the Ruby community.
  • Provide insights, Inspire and equip Ruby developers with the entrepreneurial mindset and skills necessary to build successful tech startups.
  • Elevate the status of the Ruby community in Africa to become a globally recognized and integrated community.
