ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to RubyConfAfrica 2025

This year’s theme, “Beyond code: Innovating for the future”, highlights how Ruby and open-source technologies can shape tomorrow’s world. Join us in Kenya for a two-day experience that brings together thought leaders, innovators, and entrepreneurs driven by a passion for Ruby and its power to spark meaningful change. Dive into forward-thinking discussions, hands-on workshops, and vibrant networking sessions that go beyond code — into the heart of innovation.
  • Discover how Ruby is powering innovation in open-source communities and tech ecosystems.
  • Participate in practical workshops and insightful talks from industry visionaries.
  • Connect with a passionate network of developers, founders, and changemakers shaping the future.
