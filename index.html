ABOUT EVENT

Welcome to RubyConfAfrica 2025

This year's theme celebrates Ruby’s adaptability across diverse applications. The summit brings together developers, startups, and tech leaders to explore how Ruby’s elegant syntax, strong conventions, and versatile ecosystem continue to drive innovation. Join us to exchange ideas, share best practices, and strengthen the Ruby community in Africa and beyond.
  • Explore Ruby’s role in modern software development, from web to emerging technologies.
  • Learn from experts about best practices, tools, and methodologies that enhance productivity.
  • Engage with a vibrant community shaping the future of Ruby worldwide.

WHY ATTEND THE CONFERENCE?
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded professionals, industry experts, and potential collaborators.
  • Masterclasses: Participate in specialized sessions hosted by esteemed partners
  • Expand Your Knowledge: Attend insightful talks and workshops led by industry experts. Stay ahead of the curve with the latest trends, tools, and best practices in Ruby and Software development.
  • Exhibitions: Explore cutting-edge ideas and technologies showcased by various exhibitors.

Join us on the 18th to 19th July 2025, as leading Ruby developers from around the world come together in Nairobi, Kenya, to share, inspire, and learn.
